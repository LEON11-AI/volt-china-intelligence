Conclusion: Opportunities & Challenges for BYD Intelligent Driving

BYD has successfully achieved intelligent driving strategic transformation, from cautious observation in 2024 to "Universal Smart Driving" in 2025. Through ¥100 billion investment and 5,000-person team building, BYD completed the leap from technology reserve to market explosion in just one year.

The "technology inclusion" model is of disruptive significance. BYD reduced intelligent driving costs by 30-40% through vertical integration and scale effects, achieving the breakthrough of equipping high-level intelligent driving in ¥70K-level models.

In the future, intelligent driving will become the core battlefield of automotive industry competition. With its comprehensive advantages in technology, cost, and market, BYD is expected to occupy a favorable position in this transformation, bringing more intelligent, safe, and convenient travel experiences to global consumers.
