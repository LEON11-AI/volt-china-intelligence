Conclusion: BYD's Strategic Advantage

BYD has successfully executed a strategic pivot from "Observer" to "Leader" in the intelligent driving space. By leveraging a ¥100 billion investment and a 5,000-strong engineering corps, BYD has compressed a decade of evolution into a single year of explosive growth.

The "Technology Inclusion" model is disruptive. By driving down costs by 30-40% through vertical integration, BYD has democratized high-level safety and autonomy features, making them accessible in entry-level vehicles.

As intelligent driving becomes the new battleground for automotive supremacy, BYD's synthesis of scale, cost, and technology positions it to dominate the next era of mobility.
