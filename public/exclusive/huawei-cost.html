Key Insights

Fundamental Technology Differences

Huawei ADS 3.0 adopts a LiDAR+Vision fusion approach, while Tesla HW 4.0坚持 a pure vision strategy. This reflects different philosophies on autonomous driving technology development.

Cost Control Breakthrough

Surprisingly, despite including expensive LiDAR ($420), Huawei's system cost ($1,650-2,500) is converging with Tesla's pure vision solution ($1,380-2,070) through supply chain optimization.

Safety Redundancy No Longer Expensive

Huawei has achieved the breakthrough of "safety redundancy without premium pricing". The LiDAR+Vision fusion solution shows strong competitiveness in both safety and cost-effectiveness.

Future Trend Outlook

As LiDAR costs continue to decrease, fusion solutions will become more competitive. Huawei's strategy may have advantages in complex road conditions compared to Tesla's pure vision approach.
